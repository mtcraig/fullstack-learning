Retail Bank - Data & Analytics

Data Analyst / Consultant

Working as part of an all consultant sub-team within Data & Analytics to deliver, within a timeframe of just three months, a tactical solution capable of meeting the institutions regulatory obligations for the HMT Breathing Space programme.

This involved building out a series of SAS Enterprise Guide projects and gathering information for requests from high risk customers on all relevant product holdings including MTAs, Overdrafts and Credit Cards within core brands to identify accounts and pass details to Financial Health & Support to apply protections to customers and provide them appropriate windows of time to work with their financial advisors to come to suitable future financial arrangements.

One of my key contributions was the Executioner aspect which handled process and case management, ensuring timely processing of customers within SLA and generating new files for FH&S to process daily.

Enterprise Guide Studio Python Tableau Power BI Snowflake DataGrip Hadoop / Hue Microsoft SQL Server Teradata

Developing approaches across Teradata and Snowflake SQL to aid in delivering Cost of Living support to vulnerable customers and using visualisation platforms such as Power BI to deliver a number of deep dives to key management stakeholders to improve decision making around targeted customer support, along with additional analysis and dashboarding for longer term metric tracking of key populations and assessing programme successes against desired outcomes.

Addressing the need to implement an ongoing review strategy of Overdraft limits based on customer affordability, working with Unsecured Lending to deliver a tactical solution for applying semi-automated Overdraft Limit Reductions, using SAS Studio, Snowflake and Teradata to derive cohorts based on ability to reduce the limits, calculate new limits and engage with customers where possible. My role involved completion of development, ongoing running and process maintenance as requirements were refined between the process MVP and the succeeding Strategic solution, deliver updating cohorts weekly to reduce Overdraft limits in a risk managed rollout across the customer base, including multiple Pilots and a phased MVP solution.

Development of dashboards in Power BI to track pilot outcomes for the Limit reductions work to provide insights into managing risk for future pilots and project phases, such as the long-term Strategic rollout.

Tableau dashboard support to ensure continued compliance in regulatory reporting following a re-platforming of the customer alerts system to Snowflake.

Ongoing development and maintenance of the HMT Breathing Space process in SAS EG to support evolving operational requirements, including implementation of a move from an email led data ingestion process to an API driven one, and making procedural changes to support the needs of downstream teams until such time as the Strategic process could take over - a period that was originally intended to last c. 6 months but ended up lasting over 12 months.

Delivered demonstrations to the client team on practices to aid adoption of Git into the regular workflow, and optimisations to best make use of the tools offered in GitLab to aid reviews, issue tracking, development goals, and release candidates of project code
